Privacy Policy

FindLostGadget does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

Bluetooth & Location Permissions
This app requires Bluetooth and Location permissions solely to scan nearby Bluetooth devices and estimate their distance. Location data is never collected, stored, or transmitted.

Data Collection
FindLostGadget does not collect any personal information, analytics data, or usage data.

Data Sharing
No user data is shared with third parties.

In-App Purchases
The app offers a one-time premium purchase to unlock additional features. All payments are securely processed by Google Play. The app does not store or access any payment information.

Security
Since no personal data is collected or stored, there is no risk of data exposure from the app itself.

Changes
This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
